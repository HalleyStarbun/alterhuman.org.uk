Mental Health

If you or someone you know is experiencing an urgent mental health crisis, please dial 999 immediately

Samaritans

Open 24/7, 365 days a year

Phone: 116 123

Email: jo@samaritans.org (May take 24+ hours for a response)

Mind

Open 9am-6pm weekdays (except bank holidays)

Phone: 0300 102 1234

Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM)

Open 5pm-midnight, 365 days a year

Phone: 0800 585858

Webchat: CALM Webchat

Shout

Open 24/7

Text: 85258

Website: giveusashout.org

NHS 111 (Option 2)

Open 24/7, 365 days a year

Phone: 111

Website: 111.nhs.uk

Switchboard LGBT+

Open 10am-10pm, 365 days a year

Phone: 0800 0119100

Email: hello@switchboard.lgbt (Aims to reply within five days)

Webchat: https://switchboard.lgbt/contact-us

While this is not a complete list of all availible services, these should be a good starting point for everyone. Some services offer British Sign Language (BSL) or other languages.
